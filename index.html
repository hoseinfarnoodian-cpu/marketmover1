Common questions

The stuff people usually ask.

How does payment work?

Two separate payments: (1) You pay your delivery fee online (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Apple Pay) when you book to lock in your slot. (2) After I send you pickup photos and you approve the item, you e-transfer the seller directly. No upfront charges from me for the item itself.

Is HST included in the quote?

No — quotes are before tax. 13% HST is added at checkout, and your receipt has the breakdown for your records (handy if you're claiming it as a business expense). For example, a $75 quote = $75 + $9.75 HST = $84.75 total.

What if I change my mind after seeing the photos?

That's your call — you can decline the item with no payment to the seller. Because I already drove to the pickup, a $20 cancellation fee applies and the rest of your delivery fee is refunded. If the item is genuinely not as described, the full delivery fee is refunded — that's on the seller, not you.

What can't you take?

Mattresses, large 3-seat sofas and sectionals, and appliances over 100 lbs. I'm a solo operation, so I stick to items I can handle on my own. Loveseats, dressers, desks, dining sets, bikes — all good.

How far do you go?

Within 30 km of downtown Toronto — including Mississauga, Markham, Vaughan, Etobicoke, Scarborough, North York, and parts of Brampton.
